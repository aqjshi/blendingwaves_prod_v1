Personal Information We Collect and Process

When you access or use the Sites, we may collect and process:

Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact & Identity: name, email address, phone number, postal address, company/organization, job title, account login details.
  • Service & Support: correspondence, support requests, survey responses, marketing preferences.
  • Transaction & Billing: payment method, billing information, purchase history.

Information from Third Parties

  • Public and business databases, social media platforms, marketing partners.
  • Referrals or integrations—for example, when you sign up via a third‑party identity provider.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser user agent, device identifiers, operating system.
  • Usage Data: pages visited, links clicked, time and date of your visit, referrer URL, search terms.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: session cookies, persistent cookies, web beacons, and pixel tags to support login sessions, analytics, personalization, and advertising.

Sensitive Personal Information

We do not generally collect Special Category Data (e.g., health, race, religion) unless you expressly provide it (for example, in support communications). We will handle any such data in compliance with applicable laws.

Marketing

We may use your contact information to send you product updates, newsletters, and event invitations. Where required by law, we will obtain your consent before sending marketing emails. You can opt out of marketing messages at any time by following the “unsubscribe” link in our emails or contacting us directly.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Performance of Contract: to provide products, support, and billing.
  • Legal Obligation: to comply with applicable laws, such as tax or export regulations.
  • Legitimate Interests: improving our platforms, preventing fraud, direct marketing (subject to your rights).
  • Consent: for marketing communications and any processing requiring explicit consent under law.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: hosting, payment processors, analytics, CRM platforms.
  • Affiliates & Subsidiaries: for group‑wide operations and consistent service.
  • Legal & Regulatory Authorities: when required by law or to protect rights.
  • Business Transfers: in connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales (with appropriate confidentiality safeguards).

We do not sell your personal information to third‑party marketers for their own use.

International Transfer of Personal Information

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We use standard contractual clauses and other legal safeguards to ensure adequate protection.

Individual Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at customer_service@usaaat.com.

Access and Choice

You may review and update your account and communication preferences at any time via your account settings or by contacting us.

Document Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described, for legal compliance, and for legitimate business purposes. Retention periods vary by data type and applicable law.

Information Security

We implement industry‑standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

Third‑Party Links

Our Sites may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices; please review the privacy policy of each site you visit.

Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns, or wish to exercise your rights, please email us at customer_service@usaaat.com.

Updates to this Notice

We may update this Notice periodically. The revised Notice will be posted on our Sites with a new Effective Date. Where required by law, we will seek your consent for material changes.
